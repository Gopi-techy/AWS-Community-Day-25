About AWS Student Community Day

A day dedicated to learning, networking, and celebrating the AWS community

AWS Student Community Day 2025 is the premier gathering for those passionate about cloud computing and technology innovation. This event brings together aspiring cloud professionals, experienced practitioners, and industry leaders to share knowledge, build connections, and explore the latest in AWS technologies.

Join us for a day of learning, networking, and discovery as we explore the future of cloud technology together. Connect with fellow students, meet industry professionals, and be part of a community that's shaping the digital world.

Are the meals offered at free of cost?

Certainly, the food served is complimentary, along with the snacks and refreshments.

Will Goodies be provided for the participants?

Yes, the goodies will be provided for everyone.

Yes, all participants will receive a certificate of participation upon completion of the event.
