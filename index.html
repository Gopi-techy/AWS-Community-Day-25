About AWS Student Community Day

A day dedicated to learning, networking, and celebrating the AWS community

AWS Student Community Day 2025 is the premier gathering for students passionate about cloud computing and technology innovation. This event brings together aspiring cloud professionals, experienced practitioners, and industry leaders to share knowledge, build connections, and explore the latest in AWS technologies.

Whether you're just starting your cloud journey or looking to advance your skills, this event offers something for everyone. From hands-on workshops to inspiring keynotes, you'll gain practical knowledge and insights that will accelerate your career in cloud computing.

Join us for a day of learning, networking, and discovery as we explore the future of cloud technology together. Connect with fellow students, meet industry professionals, and be part of a community that's shaping the digital world.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers! Find everything you need to know about AWS Community Day 2025

What is AWS Community Day?

AWS Community Day is a full-day event organized by students for students, featuring 25+ speakers, hands-on sessions, networking opportunities, and insights from AWS experts, industry leaders, and student mentors. It's designed to help students learn cloud computing, build their careers, and connect with the AWS community.

Who can attend this event?

This event is open to all students, professionals, and anyone interested in learning about AWS and cloud technologies. Whether you're a beginner or have advanced experience, there's something for everyone across our three parallel tracks.

Is there any registration fee?

No! AWS Community Day 2025 is completely free to attend. This includes access to all sessions, lunch, refreshments, networking opportunities, AWS swag, and certificates. Registration is required to secure your spot.

What should I bring to the event?

Bring your laptop, notebook, and enthusiasm to learn! We recommend bringing business cards for networking, a power bank for your devices, and comfortable clothing. All materials and refreshments will be provided.

Still have questions?

Our team is here to help! Reach out to us for any additional queries.
