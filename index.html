About AWS Student Community Day - Chennai 2025

A day dedicated to learning, networking, and celebrating the AWS community

AWS Student Community Day - Chennai 2025 is the premier gathering for those passionate about cloud computing and technology innovation. This event brings together aspiring cloud professionals, experienced practitioners, and industry leaders to share knowledge, build connections, and explore the latest in AWS technologies.

Join us for a day of learning, networking, and discovery as we explore the future of cloud technology together. Connect with fellow students, meet industry professionals, and be part of a community that's shaping the digital world.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers! Find everything you need to know about AWS Student Community Day - Chennai 2025

Who can attend the event?

The event is open to:

  • Students (Undergraduate, Postgraduate)
  • Faculty members
  • Industry professionals
  • Startup founders
  • Tech enthusiasts

Do I need to pay to attend?

Yes. There are various ticket categories:

  • Early Bird – Student Pass
  • Student Regular Pass
  • Industry Professional Passes
  • Group Passes

All tickets include full-day access, food/snacks, goodies, and entry to all sessions and networking activities.

Will I get a certificate for attending?

E-certificates will be provided. This event focuses on real-time interaction, learning, and networking value.

Is food provided at the event?

Yes, all passes include lunch, snacks, and refreshments throughout the day.

Can I attend if I'm from a different college/university?

Yes! Students from any institution can participate. It's an open event.

How do I stay updated?

Yes, the goodies will be provided for everyone.
